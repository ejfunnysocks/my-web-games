Guide

Powerups

  • ❤Life — gain an extra life.
  • ✨Star Rush — more stars spawn for a while.
  • 🧯Calm — fewer bombs spawn for a while.
  • 🛡Shield — deflect up to 3 bombs.
  • ↔Wide — paddle is wider for a while.

How to play

  • Use ← → or A / D to move.
  • Catch stars, avoid bombs.
  • Powerups drop roughly every 25 stars that fall.
  • Stars you earn add to Total stars so you can unlock new paddle colors.
