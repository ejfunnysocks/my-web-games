🚀 Cannon Crush Guide

Goal

Defend your base by shooting enemy balls before they reach you. Clear all the balls in each level to advance, and defeat boss rounds every 5 levels.

Controls

Move: Arrow keys or A / D | Shoot: Hold W, ↑, or Space (auto-fires while held). On mobile, drag the slider to move and tap the fire button to shoot.

How it works

Balls fly in from either side. Each ball has HP shown on it — shoot it enough times to destroy it. Spend coins earned from kills on upgrades like damage, fire rate, multi-shot, piercing bullets, and more. Boss rounds spawn a giant enemy that slams down at you and sends minion balls!
