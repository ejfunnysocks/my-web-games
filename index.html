📖 How to Play

🚀 Cannon Crush
Goal

Defend your base by shooting enemy balls before they reach you. Clear all the balls in each level to advance, and defeat boss rounds every 5 levels.

Controls

Move: Arrow keys or A / D  |  Shoot: Hold W, ↑, or Space (auto-fires while held). On mobile, drag the slider to move and tap the fire button to shoot.

How it works

Balls fly in from either side. Each ball has HP shown on it — shoot it enough times to destroy it. Spend coins earned from kills on upgrades like damage, fire rate, multi-shot, piercing bullets, and more. Boss rounds spawn a giant enemy that slams down at you and sends minion balls!

⭐ Star Catcher
Goal

Move your paddle to catch falling stars and score points. Avoid bombs — they cost you a life. You start with 3 lives.

Controls

Move: Arrow keys or A / D. On mobile, drag the slider at the bottom of the game area.

How it works

Stars and bombs fall from the top of the screen, speeding up over time. Every 25 stars that fall, a powerup drops. Powerups include: ❤ extra life, ✨ Star Rush (more stars), 🧯 Calm (fewer bombs), 🛡 Shield (deflects up to 3 bombs), and ↔ Wide (wider paddle). Catching stars also builds your total star count, which unlocks new paddle colors in the main menu.

🌀 Vortex Voyager
Goal

Survive as long as possible while plummeting down a procedurally generated neon tunnel. Dodge the red wall segments — touching one ends your run.

Controls

Rotate the tunnel: Click and drag left/right, press ← / → arrow keys, or touch and swipe. Your ship stays fixed at the bottom center — you rotate the world instead.

How it works

The tunnel rings scroll toward you continuously, getting faster as your distance increases. Obstacle rings have red jagged wall segments that block part of the ring's opening — rotate so the gap lines up with your ship's position (bottom center). Your score increases for every ring you pass through.

🥷 Ninja Dash
Goal

Run as far as possible. The ninja runs automatically — your only job is to flip gravity to dodge spikes. Get a new personal best distance to set a record shown on the side bar.

Controls

Flip gravity: Tap / click anywhere, or press Space. Every flip instantly teleports the ninja to the opposite wall (top or bottom).

How it works

Spikes are attached to either the top or bottom wall. The ninja's running speed increases over time, making spikes come faster and giving you less reaction time. There's no health — one spike hit and it's over. Your distance is tracked in meters and saved as your personal best.

🔫 Frag Duel (1v1 Multiplayer)
Goal

Frag your opponent to win the duel. This is a real-time multiplayer game using Peer-to-Peer technology.

Controls

Move: WASD  |  Jump: Space  |  Aim/Shoot: Mouse (Click to shoot)  |  Lock Mouse: Click anywhere during play.

Multiplayer Link

One player clicks Host and receives an ID. Copy that ID and send it to your opponent. The second player pastes the ID into the Join box and clicks connect.
