🌀 Vortex Voyager

Goal

Survive as long as possible while plummeting down a procedurally generated neon tunnel. Dodge the red wall segments — touching one ends your run.

Controls

Rotate the tunnel: Click and drag left/right, press ← / → arrow keys, or touch and swipe. Your ship stays fixed at the bottom center — you rotate the world instead.

How it works

The tunnel rings scroll toward you continuously, getting faster as your distance increases. Obstacle rings have red jagged wall segments that block part of the ring's opening — rotate so the gap lines up with your ship's position (bottom center). Your score increases for every ring you pass through.
